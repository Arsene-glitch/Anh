Male Uniform Measurements

In ROTC uniform play a really big part in your grade so try and keep your uniform as clean and keep the measurements as on point as you can.

Class B Measurements

Name tag:Centered between the top of the pocket and the pocket button.

Rank: On shoulder tab.

Ribbon:Centered on the pocket and stacked up from the top of the pocket.

Wreath: 1/8 inch above the top of the pocket and centered (left-right) with the pocket.

The star is worn inside the wreath.

Arc: Centered between the bottom of the pocket flap and the bottom of the pocket. 1/8 inch for each.

Cord: Only 2 can be worn at once. If only 1 is worn, cord goes to the left in shoulder button.

Class A Measurements

Name tag:>Centered on the pocket and stacked up from the top of the pocket.

Rank: Center Rank Insignia on shoulder lip 5/8 inch from outside shoulder seam.

Ribbon:Centered on the pocket and stacked up from the top of the pocket.

ROTC insignia: 5/8 inch above the notch on both collars, with the centerline of the insignia bisecting the notch and parallel to the inside edge of the lapel.

Torch of Knowledge: On both lapels 1 1/4 inches below the ROTC insignia, with the centerline of the insignia bisecting the ROTC insignia and parallel to the inside edge of the lapel.

Wreath: 1/8 inch above the top of the pocket and centered (left-right) with the pocket.

The star is worn inside the wreath.

Arc: Centered between the bottom of the pocket flap and the bottom of the pocket. 1/8 inch for each.

Cord: Only 2 can be worn at once. If only 1 is worn, cord goes to the left in shoulder button. Female Uniform

Additional Requirement

Clean-cut hair cuts, off ear and collar.

No facial hair

No earrings or piercings of any kind worn with the uniform.

only black sock should be worn with uniform. Sock must be calf length.
