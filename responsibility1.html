Batallion Responsibility

Battalion Commander(BC) is the highest rank you could earn within the battalion, as a battalion commander you are responsible for the entire battalion. Every mistake is yours and yours alone, you also will be in charge of event such as ceremonies and parade.With that being said you decide everything happen going on the battalion. Without a doubt this is the heaviest role you can earn in the battalion. Do you have what it take to be one?

Executive Officer(XO) is the second highest rank you can earn within the battalion, as executive officer you are in charge of the staff and what the staff does, in the absent of the Batalion Commander, you will be in charge of the battalion. You will also have to do alot of report so just be prepare :)

Command Sergeant Major (CSM), as a CSM you will need to check in with Color Guard a lot and make sure they are ready. You will also help train Cadet in term of appearence, military courtesy and discipline.

Administration Officer (S1), as S1 you are responsible for roaster of the batalion, you will need to do report and along with list for event and keeping the bullet board up to dates.

Security Officer (S2), as S2 you are responsible for for keeping the rifle in check, regularly checking the condition of the equipment and write down any that is damage.

Operations Officer (S3), as S3 you are in charge of team training schedule. You will also need to plan, organize and supervise Cadet training.

Supply Officer (S4), as S4 you are in charge of coordinates for, receives, distributes and controls supplies and equipment for training and keeping in check of supplies.

Public Affairs Officer (S5), as S5 you are in charge the batalion newspaper, keeping in touch with the army recruit and updating the batalion social media.

Financial Officer (S7), as S7 you are in charge of funding and fundraiser.
