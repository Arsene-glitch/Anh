Archery

What is it about?

In archery you will learn how to shoot a bow and arrow. That might seem easy at first but you will need to have good focus and shooting style.
But don't stress! because in archery they will teach you how to prefecting your shotting skil.

Requirement in joining

In order to be in the archery team, you will need to try out. If you pass the try out then you will either be place in JV(Junior Varsity) or varisty Team.

What's the perk in joining Academic

Once you compete for Archery, you will get a arc, a cord and a medal depend on your skill at shooting!

What are the practice hours?

There will be practice everyday for an hour only during archery season.

Disclaimer: Although it might seem easy, pulling back the bow can be quite hard, you are welcome to try but if you get hurt, just brush it off :)
